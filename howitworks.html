How It Works

The Steps

Experience the luxury of our state‑of‑the‑art spray parfum vending machine. Designed for precision and elegance, our machine delivers your chosen fragrance with sophistication.

  1. Touch the screen: Engage with our interactive display.
  2. Select your fragrance: Choose from our curated collection of luxurious scents.
  3. Set your spray amount: Customize the dosage for your perfect experience.
  4. Make your payment: Complete a secure and seamless transaction.
  5. Position yourself: Extend your hand or neck towards the nozzle.
  6. Press the illuminated button: Activate the spray mechanism.
  7. Enjoy your scent: Revel in the exquisite fragrance that accompanies you.
